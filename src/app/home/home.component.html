GasRadar

The fuel finding assistant you can rely on

About GasRadar?

The GasRadar app is a GPS-based application program developed for smartphones and tablets which provides prices and business details of nearby fuel stations, Supermarkets and Spaza shops from user-submitted data, and directly from station operators. The app has an Android version only for now.

This app focuses on helping the communities locate LPG Gas outlets near them at reasonable prices - as we are facing a lot of load shedding in South Africa lately. We also help our users locate nearby fuel stations near them and even in remote areas.

Our Customers?

Our clients are from the deep townships of SOWETO. The small businesses at the corner of the street, the fuel garages/ stations and Supermarkets in the locations in and around Soweto. The customers/ users of our app are also from the locations and abroad throughout the other areas outside Soweto and even on other provinces in SA. These are people who use gas whether to cook or light up their homes, cars etc - whatever they use it for they always need to find it.
